Our vision

Invest in students, not against us.

Ethical investment, affordability, student power, and campus life — open a row for detail.

Adopt an Ethical Investment Policy — divest from harmful holdings and free funds for student needs.
Work with UCSA, UC Divest Coalition, and Students for Justice in Palestine to amend UC investment policy: fossil fuels, arms, tobacco, detention, surveillance, and firms complicit in severe human rights violations.

Affordability & equity

Free 2-hour student parking in every structure.
Establish free student-designated (SD) parking spots in every parking structure.
End parking enforcement ticketing quotas.
End unfair quotas and ticketing that burden students and commuters; respect professional discretion for enforcement staff.
Affordable options in 24-hour markets; extend hours campus-wide.
Work with AS and administration on affordable essentials and 24/7 market hours aligned with how students actually live and study.
Expand emergency housing programs.
More funding, time, and relocation support for Student Rapid Rehousing.

Accountability & democracy

End the admin veto on Associated Students.
Move ASUCSD toward 501(c)(3) independence so students run programs and spaces with real autonomy — a UC-wide solidarity effort.
Let students put policy on the ballot.
UCSD Students’ Initiative: ballot measures by signature, no paid circulation, threshold tied to undergraduate turnout.
UC Students’ Assembly — deliberate with leadership.
Annual summit: lottery-selected students meet Regents, chancellors, and stakeholders on critical issues.
End UCSDPD collaboration with ICE and DHS.
Ban data sharing and financial collaboration; address surveillance vendor pipelines that reach DHS.

Campus experience

Make professors learn to teach.
Mandate pedagogy training so courses are actionable, relevant, and accessible.
More TAs, better ratios.
Cap TA-to-student ratios so graduate workers can actually support undergrads.
End quarterly re-verification of disabilities.
Stop the paywall to accommodations; align with fairer federal timelines.
